## Terrain Features

### Row 1 (North Wall)
- **A1-C1**: Building wall with windows (partial cover)
- **D1-F1**: Open alley space
- **G1-J1**: Building wall with door at H1

### Row 2 (Center Alley)
- **A2-B2**: Trash bins and boxes (difficult terrain)
- **C2-E2**: Open cobblestone
- **F2-H2**: Open cobblestone
- **I2-J2**: Wooden crates (cover, can be climbed)

### Row 3 (South Wall)
- **A3-D3**: Building wall (solid)
- **E3-G3**: Recessed doorway (Professor Maellin hidden at F3)
- **H3-J3**: Building wall with small windows

---

## Movement Notes

- Each square = 10 feet
- Normal movement = 30 feet (3 squares)
- Dash = 60 feet (6 squares)
- Difficult terrain = half movement
- Climbing crates = 10 feet movement per square
