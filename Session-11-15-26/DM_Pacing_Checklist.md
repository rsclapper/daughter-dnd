## Speedup Techniques (If Running Behind)

### General
- [ ] Montage transitions instead of detailed scenes
- [ ] Give information without requiring rolls
- [ ] Streamline NPC dialogues (key points only)
- [ ] Skip optional content (Hall of Archmages, travel encounter)

### Combat Specific
- [ ] Reduce enemy HP by 25%
- [ ] Enemies flee at 50% instead of fighting to death
- [ ] Use 60-second turn timer
- [ ] Describe multiple attacks at once
- [ ] Skip low-importance combatants (zealots flee/surrender)

### Roleplay Specific
- [ ] "While you're thinking, let me move us to..."
- [ ] Interrupt long discussions with events
- [ ] Give clear time limits: "You have 5 minutes before X happens"

---

## Slowdown Techniques (If Running Ahead)

### General
- [ ] Add detailed descriptions
- [ ] More NPC interactions
- [ ] Optional side content (Hall of Archmages)
- [ ] Let players drive (ask open questions)

### Combat Specific
- [ ] Add reinforcements (more enemies)
- [ ] Increase enemy HP by 25%
- [ ] Environmental complications (fire spreads faster)
- [ ] Enemies use smarter tactics

### Roleplay Specific
- [ ] "Does anyone want to add anything?"
- [ ] "What's your character thinking?"
- [ ] Allow deeper conversations with NPCs
