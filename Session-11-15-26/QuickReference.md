## Act Structure

### **Act 1: Politics and Pressure** (2-3 hours)

**Morning: Victory Ceremony** (10 min)
- Location: Embercross town square
- Crowd cheering, merchants offering gifts
- Quick celebratory scene
- Transition: Council summons delivered

**Midday: Council of Three Meeting** (45-60 min)
- **File**: `Encounters/Council_Meeting.md`
- **NPCs**: Thrane, Mirabel, Darius (+ Professor Maellin outside)
- **Objective**: Three factions want the Codices
- **Outcome**: Party chooses faction or keeps Codices
- **Key Decision**: Sets up rest of campaign

**Afternoon: Codex Map Revelation** (15 min)
- Codices project holographic map
- Shows Fractured Seal location (15 miles NE)
- Cryptic text: "WHERE THREE BECAME ONE, THE PRISON WEAKENS"
- Builds urgency

**Evening: Free Time** (30 min)
- Shopping, research, rest
- Professor Maellin may warn about Varn
- The Bard provides Fractured Seal info
- Party prepares for journey

---

### **Act 2: Attack and Decision** (1-2 hours)

**Midnight: Assassination Attempt** (30-45 min combat)
- **File**: `Encounters/Assassination_Attempt.md`
- **Map**: `Maps/Combat_Map_Inn.md`
- **Enemies**: Midnight Blade + team (CR 9-10 encounter)
- **Objective**: Varn's assassins try to steal Codices
- **Outcome**: Party survives, finds evidence against Varn

**Post-Combat: Investigation** (15 min)
- Interrogate captured assassins
- Examine evidence (contract, cult tattoos)
- Link to Varn established
- Flameguard responds (Thrane arrives)

**Morning After: Decision Point** (30 min)
- Discuss what to do with Codices
- Prepare for journey to Fractured Seal
- Gather supplies, allies
- The Bard offers to come along

---

### **Act 3: The Fractured Seal** (2-3 hours)

**Afternoon: Journey** (15 min)
- 15 mile trek to ruins
- Optional travel encounter
- Build tension and atmosphere
- Arrive at ancient ravine

**Discovery: The Ruins** (30 min exploration)
- **File**: `Encounters/Fractured_Seal.md`
- **Map**: `Maps/Combat_Map_Seal.md`
- Three chambers to explore
- Glyphs tell story of Great Binding
- Hall of Twelve Archmages
- Research and investigation

**The Seal: Main Puzzle** (30 min)
- Three keyholes, party has two Codices
- Discover third Codex is missing (Varn has it!)
- Decision: Insert both, one, or neither
- Consequences based on choice

**Guardian Awakens: Boss Fight** (45-90 min combat)
- **File**: `Encounters/Seal_Guardian.md`
- **Guardian CR**: 10 (deadly for level 7-8 party)
- Difficulty varies by party choice
- Can be negotiated with or fought
- Epic climactic battle

**Cliffhanger: Entity's Message** (15 min)
- Codices placed (or not)
- Entity speaks directly to party
- "Two keys returned. One remains with my prophet."
- Session ends on dramatic note

## Critical Plot Points

### The Nine Seals System
1. Ancient Fire Entity imprisoned in nine locations
2. Nine Codices act as keys/locks for the seals
3. Party has two Codices (from previous adventures)
4. Fractured Seal is PRIMARY seal, needs THREE Codices
5. Third Codex stolen by Varn family 50 years ago

### Varn's Master Plan
1. Collect all nine Codices
2. Bring them together at Prime Seal
3. Break the binding, free the Entity
4. Become Entity's vessel (he thinks)
5. Actually: Entity will consume him

### Time Pressure
- **If both Codices inserted**: 2-4 years before seal fails
- **If one Codex inserted**: Weeks to months
- **If none inserted**: Seal failing now
- Creates urgency for finding third Codex

### The Big Revelation
- Varn (or his grandfather) stole the third Codex 50 years ago
- Evidence found at Fractured Seal (graffiti)
- Next campaign arc: Confront Varn, retrieve Codex
- Sets up Sessions 2-5+

## Combat Quick Reference

### Assassination Attempt

**Enemies**:
- Midnight Blade: AC 17, HP 95, Sneak Attack +4d6
- Raven (Sniper): AC 16, HP 65, Longbow +8, Poison
- Wraith: AC 15, HP 45, Stealth specialist
- Crusher: AC 16, HP 85, Sentinel feat
- Zealots (2): AC 13, HP 30 each, Fanatics

**Tactics**: Stealth infiltration, grab Codices, escape
**Retreat**: At 50% HP or 2+ team down
**Map**: `Maps/Combat_Map_Inn.md`

### Seal Guardian

**Base Stats**: AC 18, HP 200, Large construct
**Resistances**: All nonmagical weapons
**Immunities**: Poison, psychic, fire, many conditions
**Regeneration**: 16 HP/turn (from crystal veins)

**Difficulty Variants**:
- **Both Codices Inserted**: Moderate (tests, doesn't kill)
- **One Codex Inserted**: Hard (concerned, forceful)
- **None Inserted**: Deadly (hostile, full power + elementals)

**Special**: Can be negotiated with mid-combat
**Map**: `Maps/Combat_Map_Seal.md`

## Decision Points & Consequences

### Council Meeting Choice

**Option 1: Flameguard Vault**
- Pro: Safety, 5,000 gp, protection
- Con: Static target, insider threat possible
- Ally: Thrane
- Oppose: Darius, possibly Mirabel

**Option 2: Return to Fractured Seal**
- Pro: Strengthens seal, right thing to do
- Con: Lose access to Codices
- Ally: Mirabel, The Bard
- Oppose: Darius wants destruction

**Option 3: Church Destruction**
- Pro: Permanent solution (allegedly)
- Con: WILL FAIL (Codices can't be destroyed)
- Ally: Darius
- Oppose: Mirabel (warns it will break seal)
- Result: Dramatic failure, must choose new option

**Option 4: Keep Them**
- Pro: Party maintains control
- Con: ALL factions oppose, political pressure
- Result: Harder to operate in Embercross

### Fractured Seal Choice

**Insert Both Codices**
- Buys 2-4 years
- Guardian respectful
- Quest: Find third Codex in time
- Best ending for world

**Insert One Codex**
- Buys weeks/months
- Guardian concerned
- Keep one Codex (still hunted)
- Middle ground

**Insert Neither**
- Seal continues failing
- Guardian hostile
- Party keeps both (selfish choice)
- Hardest Guardian fight

## Improvisation Tools

### If Party Goes Off-Script

**"We want to confront Varn NOW!"**
- He has alibi (trade conference in other city)
- Cannot be found currently
- Warehouse raid possible (cult cell)
- Set up future confrontation

**"We want to find the third Codex immediately!"**
- Fractured Seal provides lead (Varn has it)
- Need to plan assault on Varn's holdings
- Cannot skip Guardian encounter (blocks exit)
- Becomes next session arc

**"We don't trust any faction!"**
- Valid choice! All three have agendas
- Creates political tension
- Party independence maintained
- Harder path but valid

**"Can we destroy the seal to free the Entity?"**
- Guardian stops them violently
- The Bard: "That's literally the apocalypse!"
- Mirabel appears to intervene
- Bad idea, redirect gently

### If Combat Going Wrong

**Assassination Too Easy**:
- Reinforcements arrive (2 more zealots)
- Midnight Blade fights smarter
- Environmental hazards (fire)

**Assassination TPK Risk**:
- Flameguard arrives early (Round 5)
- Assassins achieve objective (steal Codex) and flee
- NPC ally helps (Bard, Maellin, Mirabel)

**Guardian Too Easy**:
- Increase HP to 250
- Add stone elemental reinforcements
- Use all Legendary Actions
- Phase 2 transformation

**Guardian TPK Risk**:
- Guardian yields early (proven worthy)
- Takes Codices, lets party live
- NPC ally helps
- Reduce HP to 150

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Can the party split the Codices?**
A: Yes, but weakens both seals more. Not recommended in-world.

**Q: What if they give Codices to all three factions?**
A: Impossible - only two Codices. Creative diplomacy could work for one.

**Q: Can Professor Maellin be cured?**
A: Yes! Stop his Codex research + Greater Restoration. Good quest hook.

**Q: Is Varn present this session?**
A: No, he's the puppet master. Sends assassins. Direct confrontation later.

**Q: Can Guardian be permanent ally?**
A: Yes, if party proves worthy. Can be summoned once via ritual later.

**Q: What if party romance NPCs?**
A: Your game! The Bard is single and charming. Mirabel is 127 years old. Your call.
