# Combat Log: Professor Chase Ambush

**Location**: Dark alley behind the Council Chamber
**Encounter Type**: Ambush
**Party Level**: Assumed Level 5
**Enemies**: 3 Assassins (CR 8 encounter)

---

## Initiative Order
1. **Druid** - 22
2. **Rogue** - 11
3. **Bard** - 11
4. **Assassin 1** - 10
5. **Assassin 2** - 9
6. **Assassin 3** - 7
7. **Paladin** - 4

---

## Round 1

### Druid (Initiative 22)
- **Action**: Wild Shape into Brown Bear
- **Movement**: Moves 10 feet toward Assassin 1
- **Result**: Now has 34 HP, AC 11, powerful melee attacks

### Rogue (Initiative 11)
- **Surprise Condition**: Party was surprised, Rogue acts normally
- **Action**: Sneak Attack with shortsword against Assassin 2
- **Attack Roll**: 18 to hit
- **Damage**: 1d6+3 piercing + 3d6 sneak attack = 16 damage
- **Movement**: Uses Cunning Action to Dash behind cover

### Bard (Initiative 11)
- **Action**: Casts *Faerie Fire* (DEX save DC 14)
- **Targets**: All 3 assassins
- **Results**:
  - Assassin 1: Fails save (outlined in light)
  - Assassin 2: Succeeds save
  - Assassin 3: Fails save (outlined in light)
- **Effect**: Assassins 1 & 3 glow, attacks against them have advantage

### Assassin 1 (Initiative 10) - OUTLINED
- **Target**: Druid (Bear form)
- **Attack 1**: Shortsword - 12 to hit (disadvantage from Faerie Fire) - MISS
- **Attack 2**: Shortsword - 15 to hit - HIT for 7 piercing damage
- **Bear HP**: 34 - 7 = 27 HP remaining

### Assassin 2 (Initiative 9) - WOUNDED
- **Target**: Rogue (in cover)
- **Attack 1**: Shortsword with disadvantage (cover) - 11 to hit - MISS
- **Attack 2**: Shortsword with disadvantage (cover) - 8 to hit - MISS
- **Movement**: Moves to better position

### Assassin 3 (Initiative 7) - OUTLINED
- **Target**: Bard
- **Attack 1**: Shortsword - Natural 20! CRITICAL HIT
- **Damage**: 2d6+3 = 11 piercing damage
- **Attack 2**: Shortsword - 14 to hit - HIT for 6 piercing damage
- **Total to Bard**: 17 damage

### Paladin (Initiative 4)
- **Action**: Divine Smite attack on Assassin 3
- **Attack Roll**: 16 to hit (with advantage from Faerie Fire)
- **Damage**: 1d8+3 slashing + 2d8 divine smite = 14 damage
- **Assassin 3 Status**: Heavily wounded but standing

---

## Round 2

### Druid (Brown Bear)
- **Action**: Multiattack against Assassin 1
- **Bite Attack**: 15 to hit (with advantage) - HIT for 8 piercing damage
- **Claw Attack**: 17 to hit (with advantage) - HIT for 7 slashing damage
- **Assassin 1 Status**: Bloodied but fighting

### Rogue
- **Action**: Sneak Attack on Assassin 2 (flanking with bear)
- **Attack Roll**: 19 to hit
- **Damage**: 1d6+3 + 3d6 sneak attack = 18 damage
- **Result**: Assassin 2 drops unconscious
- **Movement**: Moves to engage Assassin 3

### Bard
- **Action**: Casts *Healing Word* on self as bonus action (regain 7 HP)
- **Action**: *Vicious Mockery* on Assassin 1 (WIS save DC 14)
- **Result**: Assassin fails save, takes 6 psychic damage and has disadvantage on next attack

### Assassin 1 - OUTLINED, BLOODIED, MOCKED
- **Target**: Druid (Bear)
- **Attack 1**: Shortsword with disadvantage - 8 to hit - MISS
- **Attack 2**: Shortsword with disadvantage - 11 to hit - MISS
- **Status**: Clearly struggling

### Assassin 2 - UNCONSCIOUS
- **Death Save 1**: 12 - Success (1 success, 0 failures)

### Assassin 3 - OUTLINED, HEAVILY WOUNDED
- **Target**: Paladin
- **Attack 1**: Shortsword - 13 to hit - MISS
- **Attack 2**: Shortsword - 16 to hit - HIT for 5 piercing damage
- **Movement**: Attempts to disengage and flee

### Paladin
- **Action**: Attack of opportunity as Assassin 3 flees
- **Attack Roll**: 18 to hit (with advantage)
- **Damage**: 1d8+3 = 7 slashing damage
- **Result**: Assassin 3 drops unconscious

---

## Round 3

### Druid (Brown Bear)
- **Action**: Grapple Assassin 1
- **Athletics Check**: 16 vs Assassin's 12
- **Result**: Assassin 1 is grappled and prone

### Rogue
- **Action**: Non-lethal attack on grappled Assassin 1
- **Attack Roll**: Automatic hit (advantage on prone target)
- **Damage**: Declares non-lethal, deals 8 damage
- **Result**: Assassin 1 falls unconscious

### Combat Ends
**Duration**: 3 rounds (18 seconds)
**Party Status**:
- Druid: 27/34 HP (in bear form)
- Rogue: Full HP
- Bard: Wounded but stable
- Paladin: Lightly wounded
- **Prisoners**: 2 unconscious assassins (1 died from blood loss)

---

## Aftermath
- Professor Maellin is found deeper in the alley, badly beaten but alive
- The assassins carry no identification but have matching tattoos (investigate later)
- Combat drew attention - city guards arrive 2 minutes later
- Party must decide what to do with prisoners and explain situation

## XP Award
**Total XP**: 2,300 (split 4 ways = 575 XP each)
**Encounter Difficulty**: Hard (appropriate for level 5 party)
