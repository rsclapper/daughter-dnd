# Captain Solric Varn - The Flamebrand Prophet
**PRIMARY VILLAIN - RETURNING from Session 9-20-25**

## Basic Info
- **Race**: Human
- **Class**: Fighter (Eldritch Knight) / Warlock (Fiend Pact) - Multiclass
- **Age**: 42
- **Height**: 6'2"
- **Build**: Powerfully built, military bearing, commanding presence
- **Title**: Captain (former military), Leader of the Flamebrand Cult
- **Wealth**: Extremely wealthy noble family, owns multiple properties

## Appearance
- Sharp, aristocratic features with a strong jawline
- Dark hair with distinctive silver streak (from pact magic)
- Intense amber eyes that seem to glow faintly in dim light
- Wears tailored noble clothing in red and black (Flamebrand colors)
- Flamebrand cult symbol (phoenix rising from ashes) on signet ring
- Multiple burn scars on arms, worn as badges of honor
- Carries rapier with ruby pommel (family heirloom, magical)

## Public Persona vs. Secret Identity

### Public Face: Respected Noble
- **Reputation**: Wealthy philanthropist and war hero
- **Business**: Shipping magnate, owns trading company
- **Social Standing**: Welcomed in high society across multiple cities
- **Political Influence**: Funding campaigns, owns city officials
- **Cover Story**: "Collector of historical artifacts"

### Secret Truth: Cult Leader
- **Real Identity**: High Prophet of the Flamebrand Cult
- **Goal**: Awaken the Ancient Fire Entity and become its vessel
- **Belief**: The Entity will grant him godlike power and immortality
- **Resources**: Private army, cult fanatics, corrupt officials
- **Network**: Cult cells in five major cities

### The Contradiction He Lives
- By day: Attends charity galas and city council meetings
- By night: Conducts blood rituals and commands assassinations
- **Cognitive Dissonance**: Believes he's saving the world through the Entity
- **Justification**: "Civilization needs a god-king to unite it"

## Personality

### Core Traits
- **Charismatic**: Natural leader, people want to follow him
- **Patient**: Has spent 20 years building toward his goal
- **Ruthless**: Will eliminate anyone who threatens his plans
- **Arrogant**: Believes he's chosen by destiny for greatness
- **Calculating**: Always three moves ahead

### Motivations
1. **Primary**: Become vessel for the Ancient Fire Entity
2. **Secondary**: Build an empire under his rule
3. **Hidden**: Prove himself superior to his dead father (family issues)
4. **Fear**: Dying forgotten and powerless like common men

### Flaws

- **Obsession**: The Entity whispers to him, driving him toward madness
- **Pride**: Cannot accept others as equals, must dominate

## DM Notes

### Making Varn a Compelling Villain
**Show Don't Tell**:
- Don't have NPCs say "he's evil"
- Let his actions speak: charming in public, ruthless in private
- Create moral complexity: some of his public work IS genuinely helpful
- Make party question if they're paranoid until evidence is clear

**Build Him Gradually**:
- Session 1: Mysterious figure behind assassins
- Session 2-3: Charming noble who seems reasonable
- Session 4-5: Revealed as cult leader
- Session 6+: Personal nemesis and major threat

**Personal Stakes**:
- Have him target specific party members
- Learn their fears and exploit them
- Send personal messages, not just generic threats
- Make it personal, not just business

### Using Him to Drive Plot
- Each session, Varn's influence touches the party somehow
- Always one step ahead, forcing party to react
- Provide chances for party to turn tables
- Build to satisfying final confrontation

### Avoiding Mustache-Twirling
- Give him valid points about civilization being flawed
- Show genuine belief he's doing right thing (corrupt justice)
- Make him respect worthy opponents (the party)
- Show his human side occasionally (family, honor, regret)

### The Possession Subplot
Track Varn's possession percentage:
- Session 1-3: 20% (occasional amber eye glow)
- Session 4-6: 40% (personality darkening, nightmares)
- Session 7-9: 60% (clearly not himself, followers notice)
- Final Battle: 80%+ (barely human, Entity speaking through him)

Use this to foreshadow and create sympathy. Is he truly evil or corrupted victim?
