# Scene 2: Victory Ceremony
**Time**: 3:15 PM - 3:30 PM (15 minutes)
**Type**: Roleplay - Celebration
**Location**: Embercross Town Square

---

## Purpose
- Celebrate party's heroism
- Show city's gratitude
- Light, fun moment before tension builds
- Establish party's fame/celebrity status

---

## Scene Checklist
- [ ] Describe town square crowd and decorations
- [ ] Mayor's speech (keep brief, 2 min)
- [ ] Award presentation
- [ ] Party gets moment to speak (optional)
- [ ] Merchant gifts/offers
- [ ] Transition to Council summons

---

## Opening Description

**Read**:
> *The town square of Embercross has been transformed. Banners in crimson and gold hang from every building. The fountain in the center has been decorated with flowers. And the crowd—easily three hundred citizens—erupts in cheers as you emerge from the inn.*
>
> *Children throw flower petals. Musicians play triumphant fanfares. Merchants wave from their stalls. The air smells of fresh bread, roasted meat, and celebration.*
>
> *At the center, on a hastily-built wooden platform, stands Mayor Brennan Ashveil, flanked by city officials. He beams at your approach.*

---

## Mayor's Speech

**Read** (or paraphrase):
> **Mayor Ashveil**: *"Citizens of Embercross! Three days ago, our city faced annihilation. The Convergence Crisis threatened to tear our very reality apart. Buildings crumbled. The earth shook. We thought it was the end."*
>
> *He gestures to you.*
>
> **Mayor**: *"But these heroes—these brave souls—descended into the depths beneath our city and faced an Ancient Emberkeeper. They stopped the crisis. They saved us all!"*
>
> *The crowd roars. Someone shouts: "Heroes of Embercross!"*
>
> **Mayor**: *"Today, we honor them. Today, we celebrate life. Today, we say: thank you!"*

---

## Awards & Gifts

**Formal Award**:
- **Medal of Embercross**: Bronze medallion on crimson ribbon (worth 50 gp each as art object)
- **Honorary Citizenship**: Lifetime citizenship in Embercross (legal benefits, tax exemptions)

**Merchant Gifts** (Choose 2-3, keep brief):
1. **Tavern Owner**: "Drinks are free for life at the Wayfarer's Rest!"
2. **Blacksmith**: "Any minor repairs, no charge. You've earned it."
3. **Baker**: Basket of finest pastries (immediately consumed by crowd)
4. **Wealthy Merchant**: "Call on me if you need transport. I owe you my warehouses."

**Children**: Small handmade gifts (drawings, flower crowns, lucky stones)

---

## Party Response (Optional - 5 min max)

**Ask**: "Does anyone want to say a few words to the crowd?"

**If Yes**:
- Let each player speak briefly
- Crowd reactions:
  - Humble speech: Warm approval
  - Boastful speech: Amused cheers
  - Funny speech: Laughter and applause
  - Serious speech: Respectful silence then applause

**If No**:
- Mayor fills the gap with more praise
- Party can wave, accept gifts graciously

---

## The Summons

**After celebration peak**:

**Read**:
> *As the cheers begin to die down, a figure in formal Flameguard armor approaches the platform. She carries a sealed letter bearing three wax seals—one crimson, one gold, one silver.*
>
> *The messenger bows to you formally.*
>
> **Messenger**: *"Heroes of Embercross, the Council of Three requests your presence. They wish to discuss... the artifacts you recovered."*
>
> *Her eyes flicker to your packs, where the Codices rest.*
>
> **Messenger**: *"They await you in the Council Chamber. At your convenience... though soon would be appreciated."*
>
> *She hands you the letter and steps back.*

---

## The Letter (Handout or Read Aloud)

**Formal text**:
> *To the Heroes of Embercross,*
>
> *Your valor in ending the Convergence Crisis has earned our deepest gratitude. However, the matter of the Flamebound Codices requires immediate discussion.*
>
> *We request your presence in the Council Chamber to discuss the future of these artifacts.*
>
> *—Councilor Thrane Brightshield (Flameguard)*
> *—Mirabel, Keeper of Archives (Ember Archive)*
> *—High Priest Darius Lightbringer (Church of Eternal Light)*

---

## Crowd Dispersal

**Read**:
> *The crowd begins to disperse, still buzzing with excitement. People pat you on the back as they pass. Merchants wave. Children ask for autographs on scraps of paper.* no autographs
>
> *But you notice: several people look nervous when they see the Codices. A few make warding gestures. And in the corner of your eye, you catch Professor Maellin watching from an alley—he looks worse than before, thinner, pale. He nods once, then disappears into the crowd.*

---

## Time Check & Options

**Tell Players**:
"You've been summoned by the Council. The meeting is scheduled soon, but you have a few minutes. What do you want to do?"

**Options** (2-3 minutes each):
- Go directly to Council Chamber (Skip to Scene 3)
- Talk to someone in crowd (quick RP)
- Check on Professor Maellin (he's gone, but can investigate later)
- Discuss strategy as a party (private conversation)

**Time Limit**: "The Council is waiting, so we need to move along in about 5 minutes."

---

## Transition to Scene 3

**Read** (when ready):
> *The Council Chamber is in the civic building, just across the square. Its doors are already open, and you can see guards in Flameguard armor standing watch.*
>
> *Whatever awaits you inside, this celebration is ending. The serious business begins now.*

**Tell Players**: "Let's transition to Scene 3—the Council of Three Meeting."

---

## DM Notes

**Energy Level**: HIGH - Celebration, crowd energy
**Pace**: BRISK - Don't linger too long, this is a palate cleanser
**Music**: Triumphant, celebratory

**Purpose Reminder**: This scene is a reward and a breath before the politics begin. Let players enjoy being heroes, but keep it moving.

**Common Questions**:
- Q: "Can we sell the medals?"
  - A: "Sure, but citizens will recognize you pawned your hero medals. Might affect reputation."

- Q: "What do the three seals mean?"
  - A: "Three factions: Flameguard (military), Ember Archive (scholars), Church (religious)."

- Q: "Is Professor Maellin okay?"
  - A: "He looked terrible—thinner, pale, shaking. He didn't stay to talk."

**Time Check**: Should be done by 3:30 PM
